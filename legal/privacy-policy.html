Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 20, 2026

FeelinIt ("we," "our," or "the app") is a mental wellness application designed to help you explore and understand your emotions through interactive neuroscience-inspired visualizations. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Information We Collect

Emotion Session Data — When you explore an emotion and complete a stabilization exercise, the app records the emotion type, date, stabilization duration, and breathing quality. This data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to your personal iCloud account (Apple CloudKit) for backup and cross-device access.

Camera & Microphone — The app accesses your front camera to detect hand gestures and your microphone to detect breathing patterns during guided emotion regulation exercises. This data is processed entirely on your device in real time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

AI Conversations — Conversations with the in-app AI wellness coach ("Dr. Feel") are processed on-device using Apple's Foundation Models framework. No conversation data is sent to external servers.

Subscription Information — Subscription purchases are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your emotional journey and session history within the app.
  • To provide real-time biofeedback during emotion regulation exercises.
  • To generate contextual AI wellness coaching responses.
  • To sync your data across your devices via iCloud (if you have iCloud enabled).

3. Data Storage & Security

All personal data is stored either locally on your device or in your private iCloud container (encrypted by Apple). We do not operate external servers that store your personal data. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or disclose your personal information to third parties. No data is transmitted to external analytics services, advertising networks, or any other third-party platforms.

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following Apple platform services:

  • Apple CloudKit (iCloud) — For optional data synchronization and persistence. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit — For in-app subscription management. Governed by Apple's Terms of Service.
  • Apple Foundation Models — For on-device AI processing. No data leaves your device.

6. Children's Privacy

FeelinIt is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Your Rights

Since all your data is stored on your device and/or your personal iCloud account:

  • Access & Export — You can view all your data within the app at any time.
  • Deletion — You can delete the app to remove all local data. iCloud data can be managed through your device's iCloud settings.
  • Portability — Your data is under your control through iCloud.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected within the app and on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
